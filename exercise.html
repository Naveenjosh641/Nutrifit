Exercise

Cardio

Exercises that increase the heart rate and improve the body's use of oxygen over extended periods, such as running, swimming, and cycling.

Strength

Exercises designed to improve muscle strength and endurance through resistance, such as weightlifting and using resistance bands.

Flexibility

Exercises that enhance the range of motion of muscles and joints, such as stretching and yoga.

Balance

Exercises that improve the ability to maintain body stability and posture, such as balance beam exercises and stability ball workouts.

Hiit

A form of exercise that alternates between short, intense bursts of activity and fixed periods of less-intense activity or rest.

Plyometric

Exercises that involve explosive movements to increase power and speed, such as jump squats and box jumps.

Calisthenic

Bodyweight exercises that improve strength, flexibility, and endurance, such as push-ups, pull-ups, and squats.

Low impact

Activities that minimize stress on the joints while maintaining fitness, such as walking, swimming, and cycling.

Resistance

A broader category of strength training that includes using resistance tools like bands, weights or body weight to build muscle strength and endurance.

Stability

Exercises that enhance the muscles' ability to stabilize and support the body, often involving core strength, such as planks and balance board exercises.

Aerobic

Aerobic exercises performed in shallow water, providing low-impact resistance and improving cardiovascular and muscular fitness.
